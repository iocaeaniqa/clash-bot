**All Commands**

**__Profile Commands__**
`.profile`
Displays player statistics summary.
`.units`
Displays all laboratory upgrades and their respective max level for the current townhall level.
`.builder`
Displayer player's builder base statistics summary.
`.link <tag>`
Links discord account to the provided clash of clans player tag.
`.linked`
Displays clash of clans player tag currently linked to your discord account.
